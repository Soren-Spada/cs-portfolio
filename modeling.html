Models In Computer Science!

What Am I Modeling?

Have you ever wondered how to make the perfect rice. Well here, I have modeled the making of a perfect batch of rice. By following the instructions, created with HTML, CSS, and JS, you can make your own virtual perfect rice!

Why Is This Important?

Every expeditions, we learn a different thing. Weeks 1&2 mainly focused on the basics of HTML and CSS. This includes parts of webpages, text, images, colors, styles, and others. Weeks 3&4 focus on refining our skills with HTML and CSS along with learning the basics of JavaScipt (JS). This includes basic functions and statements, enough to create a calculator, an infopage, and a navigation bar. Our current weeks, 5&6, focus on refining our JS skills, starting with creating code for an AI in a game. Now creating our own model. In the future, I can use this information to design tutorials, recipes, or, if I get really good, engineering designs.

How To Make Rice With JavaScript

To start with the making of our virtual rice, click the bag to open the bag of rice.

For this virtual rice, we will be making 1 cup of it. Drag the measuring cup to the rice bag.

Now we have to wash the outside of the rice. Drag the rice into the bowl of water to clean it.

Good Job! After we wash the outside of the rice, we must put it through a strainer to take out the dirty water and keep the clean rice. Drag the water rice mixture to the strainer.

Now put the cleaned rice into the pot!

Get some fresh water to boil the rice. For every 1 cup of rice, use 1 1/2 cup of water. Drag the cup into the sink.

Pour your measured fresh water into the pot with the rice. Drag and Drop the water into the pot.

Press the Switch to turn the stove on High.

Press the Switch to turn the stove on low, now that the water has boiled.

Now just wait 15 minutes and you are done! Click the timer to finish up!
