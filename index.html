Why This Class Matters

Unlike most classes that have little relavance to our future lives, computer sciences brings all of the small things we learn in school, and shapes it into a skill that many companies look for. Two of the biggest majors in the Bay area is hardware and software engineering, so we are better off stating early than late. In these 1st two weeks of classes, we are learning the basics of the basics of a webpage. Once we have learned this, we can then develop the skills nessesary to create basic games and online models. Computer sciences don't only help us develop online skills, but it helps us learn to research better, and to communicate to peers all around us for help.
